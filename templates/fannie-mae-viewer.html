Risk Analyzer

Users will find an interactive tool to help gauge the risk of a loan application based on historical mortgage data. The tool considers factors such as interest rate, debt-to-income ratio, credit score, and loan amount to generate risk assessment. This tool will help investors identify the mortgage characteristics for high-risk borrowers. By investing in mortgages with lower risk borrowers, investors can get a better return on their investment.

Performance Charts

The explore page has a variety of visualizations which highlights some trends identified in the dataset. Performance charts can be used to track the overall performance of the mortgage market.
